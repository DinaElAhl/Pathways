Frequently asked questions

Can I use Pathways for free?

Yes! The Free plan includes full access to browse frameworks, apps, resources, and use the compare tool. The Curriculum Builder is available with a 3-plan limit.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for institutional plans. Payments are processed securely via Stripe.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Pro plans are month-to-month with no long-term commitment. Cancel anytime from your account settings.

Do you offer discounts for schools?

Yes! We offer volume discounts for schools and districts. Contact us via the quote form for a custom price based on your school size.

Is my data safe?

Curriculum plans are stored locally in your browser. We don't collect personal data. Institutional plans include optional cloud backup with full FERPA and GDPR-K compliance.
