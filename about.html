The E² framework

E² — "Every Student Has the Right to Understand" is the pedagogical spine behind every suggested path, lesson template, and flipped-video slot on Pathways. It rests on four pillars:

  • Lesson Design — the 5-part blueprint (Hook → Teach → Practice → Connect → Reflect).
  • Differentiation — modalities, tiered tasks, cultural responsiveness, student choice.
  • Engagement — six deliberate levers you can pull to lift engagement.
  • Classroom Culture — relationship-building, co-created norms, restorative practices.
